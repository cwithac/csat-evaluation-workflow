CLASSIFICATION  (A, B, C, or Normal)
──────────────────────────────────────────────
A (Critical):

  • Response Rate < 10%, OR
  • Score 1 % ≥ 20%

B (At Risk):
  • Avg CSAT 3.5–3.9, OR
  • Response Rate 10–19%, OR

C (Below Target):

Normal (Healthy):

When multiple classifications apply, use the worst (A > B > C > Normal).

Low:    CSAT Responses < 10, OR data is ambiguous or contradictory
